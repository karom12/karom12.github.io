Website Evaluations

Vortex

This website is not CRAP but CRAPPY because no amount of searching can help figure out what this company truly does?. It's lack of upkeep is very clearly shown. A blast from the past.

  • Contrast in the site is nonexistent where everything is brightly colored.
  • Repetition can be seen how it uses it's fonts, it's just overly filled with links.
  • It is not Aligned properly so it gives it a elementary school did this during recess vibe.
  • It lacks Proximity from it's links to regular fonts.

University of North Carolina at Charlotte

This website is an amazing example of CRAP(the good kind). It changes with the times along with being creative.

  • Contrast is very noticeable with their use of the school color for the font and background so the words are readable.
  • Repetition is out and about with the font colors and how they style information of the school.
  • Each area of the site aligned with one another to keep a consistency of information being viewed cleanly.
  • Proximity is there where there are headers that catch the readers attention with the change to smaller font for the extended information.
